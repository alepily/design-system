When the user selects the “Pay by Bank” button, the RocketShop app displays a view where the user can select the bank they wish to pay from. The user wishes to pay by their ‘Monzo’ account.

What Yapily does under the hood

Yapily provides the list of available banks that have been configured in the Dashboard.

Although Yapily does not show any UIs, Yapily offers the specific logos and icons for each of the banks in order to aid customers in creating their own interfaces.
