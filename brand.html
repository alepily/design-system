Our vision.

Core Values.

We are impatient pioneers.

Through Open Finance, we stand at the forefront of a transformative era for financial services; enablers of a better financial future.

We believe curiosity drives learning.

We are in a nascent, fast-growing category – we are always striving to learn, challenge ourselves and improve.

We obsess about quality.

As an infrastructure company, the quality of our offer and product rules. There is no compromise or room for error.

We champion fairness and honesty.

We want to transform an industry not known for openness and transparency, for the better of many.

We are surprisingly humble.

We silently power other companies, behind the scenes. Our customers are always our priority.

Using our logo.

Positioning.

Our logo is clearly spaced for clarity. Use central positioning where possible. Don’t overcrowd the surrounding space.

Colour.

The Yapily logo should only be used in black or white.

Secondary variation.

The second variation should be used as an icon, where the long logo doesn’t fit the format.

Watermark.

The emblem can be used as a watermark or as a reminder of the brand.
